Welcome to Steam Gardens!

Steam Gardens is filled to the top with tons of plants, trees, flowers, and... robots? Here at the Wooded Kingdom, the beautiful flora is taken care of by nuturing but friendly robots, and is what keeps everything happy and healthy! There is plenty to do and see here, from the towering infrastructure, the heaps of gardens, the deep woods, and more.

Check out this list of reasons why you should visit the Steam Gardens today, through the Odyssey Travel Agency!

Check Out the Museums

The Steam Gardens is home to one of the world's most popular art exhibits. Be sure to stop by for some refreshments on your adventures, currently open from 9AM to 11PM on weekdays, and 9AM to midnight on weekends.

Places To Go

With no shortage of buildings from times of long past to explore, you can be the person who gets to decide your own adventure in this mystical land!

High Tech Infrastructure

Nowhere else in the world can claim to such a high population of intelligent robots quite like Steam Gardens can. These robots are always at work, but they are more than happy to take care of all the flora! They are also quite friendly, so say hello to them if you can!

Specialized Trails

Darkness Path

The Wooded Kingdom is also home to a forest know as the Deep Woods. This special trail that runs through it has tours every hour from midnight to 3am. Are you up to it?

Flower Parkway

A perfect summertime choice, this medium length trail will have you smelling the flowers. This trail is always open.
