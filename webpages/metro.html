Welcome to New Donk City!

New Donk City Festival

Make sure you attent New Donk's Most Famous Event, the New Donk City Festival!

Held every Saturday Night, the New Donk streets are covered from head to toe in bright twinkling lights, carnival games, street musicians, and more!

The heart of the festival starts at 8pm with Mayor Pauline's Nighttime Skyline Concert! Starring New Donk City's band, Enjoy the heart of music at the biggest event in all of the Odyssey Kingdoms!

A fireworks show is held every second weekend after the concert is over. Take seats at New Donk City Square and get some sparklers to be ready when the fireworks start at 10pm!

New Donk City Square

City Square Park always has something to do! Jump Rope contests are scheduled every weekday from 12pm-6pm, alongside other pop-up events. Each weekly schedule is posted every Sunday.

Rooftop Pool

Need some serenity? New Donk City's Rooftop Pool is located on one of the largest skyscrapers in the City. Take some time away from the bustling streets and view the grand skyline.

Kong Theatre

Kong Theatre has been open for over 50 years showing plays and movies from all generations. Purchase tickets early to get a good seat!

Skyview Cafe

Skyview Cafe is New Donk's most famous restaurant! Skyview has both indoor and outdoor seating, both on ground level and on the roof!

Other Places to Go:

City Hall

New Donk City Hall is open 24/7! Come visit for refreshments, city events, and you may be lucky to catch New Donk City's local band playing a few tunes!

Shopping

There are plenty of shops all across the city! Take a walk around and support the local businesses, including the fabled Crazy Cap!

RC Racing

Want to spend some time indoors? Nothing beats racing others at our very own RC Car Race Track! Compete in tournaments or sit and watch the races, only on 23 Dixie St!
