Our Most Popular Kingdoms

Seaside Kingdom: Bubblaine

Smell the seaside breeze and feel the cool water!

Visit Seaside Kingdom's Bubblaine and relax at the warm beaches or take a dive in the beautiful ocean! You cant miss Bubblaine's Glass Sparkling Water Tower, a large elegant fountain found at the center of its waters!

Bubblaine is covered in many shallow beaches and deep waters, and you may find these natural water fountains scattered about!

Click for more about Bubblaine

Sand Kingdom: Tostarena

Need a mix of fun locals and incredible landmarks?

Take a visit to the colorful town of Tostarena! There are plenty of things to do at every corner. Say hello to the bustling musicians and townspeople, and have fun in the town square!

Don't miss Sand Kingdom's famous landmark, the Inverted Pyramid ! Nobody knows how this came to be, but it is a sight to behold.

Click for more about Tostarena

Metro Kingdom: New Donk City

Want to be where all the people and parties are?

Visit Metro Kingdom's honoured New Donk City! Walk through the busy streets with skyscrapers galore, and say hello to New Donk City's beloved Mayor Pauline. Book tickets for her amazing nighttime skyline concert!

New Donk City Square is the place to be if you always want something to do! Meet someone new at the activity-filled park!
